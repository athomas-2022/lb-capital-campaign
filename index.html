Who We Are

Our Mission

The Capital Campaign Committee exists for the continual improvement of LB Athletics — upgrading facilities, supporting general athletic programs, and funding the ongoing success of LB Athletics for generations of Eagles to come.

  • Upgrading athletic facilities across the LB campus
  • Providing general athletic program support
  • Raising self-sustaining capital for the continual growth of LB Athletics
  • Informing fellow supporters of opportunities to support LB Athletics

Our History

The Capital Campaign Committee was developed prior to 2020 as a sub-committee of the LB Athletic Boosters. Working hand in hand with the LB Athletic Boosters and the School District, the committee has helped fund multiple major athletic campus improvement projects.

Proposed Guidelines & Regulations

As the Capital Campaign Committee looks to the future, we’re working to formalize the EAGLE Foundation with clear guidelines, regulations, and governance — ensuring funds raised today create a self-sustaining endowment that supports LB Athletics for generations to come.

Next Steps in 2026

  • Formalize EAGLE Foundation governance and structure
  • Establish a $50,000 founding donation to the EAGLE Foundation
  • Direct future Stadium Videoboard advertising proceeds to the EAGLE Fund
  • Continue informing supporters of opportunities to give
